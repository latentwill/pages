Building the Ideonomy Skill
Ideonomy treats ideas like natural objects: they have dimensions, properties, edges, and neighboring forms.

Every invocation picks operators, organons, and dimension prompts so the model cannot fall back to its default brainstorming moves. latentwill/ideonomy-skill
